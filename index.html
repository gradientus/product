Grüner Veltliner

Grüner Veltliner is a white wine, made almost exclusively in Austria. It is an excellent alternative to Sauvignon Blanc or Pinot Blanc.

The most prominent fruits in Grüner Veltliner are bell peppers and limes. For some of the more mature vintages, the flavor of nectarines and peaches can also come through. Other flavors can include tarragon, ginger, any honey. The best Grüner Veltliners will have a tingly aftertaste.

Pairing

Grüner Veltliners pair well with fried dishes such as fried chicken, pork, and sausages. They also pair well with chevre, gouda, havarti, and any cheese that pairs with Riesling.
