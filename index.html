Grüner Veltliner

Grüner Veltliner is a white wine, made almost exclusively in Austria. It is an excellent alternative to Sauvignon Blanc or Pinot Blanc.

The most prominent fruits in Grüner Veltliner are bell peppers and limes. For some of the more mature vintages, the flavor of nectarines and peaches can also come through. Other flavors can include tarragon, ginger, any honey. The best Grüner Veltliners will have a tingly aftertaste.

Pairing

Grüner Veltliners pair well with fried dishes such as fried chicken, pork, and sausages. They also pair well with chevre, gouda, havarti, and any cheese that pairs with Riesling.

2009 Vintage

The weather in early 2009 was wet and cold, with budbreak setting in at a late stage. May brought fog and light rains and cooler temperatures that slowed the vines and brought massive growth.

Flowering progressed slowly in June, and into July, followed by a summer with mild temperatures and windy periods.

Autumn weather was warmer than usual and we harvested the grapes in October. The grapes showed high sugar levels. 2009 is a gorgeous vintage and wine has given rise to profound celery, herbal, and peach jam aromas.

2014 Vintage

The weather in 2014 was hot and dry, with budbreak setting in May. May brought regular rain and light intense foggy periods.

Flowering progressed into June, followed by a summer with extremely hot temperatures.

Autumn weather was warmer than usual and we harvested the grapes in October. The grapes showed average sugar levels. 2014 is dry vintage showing crip lettuce and pepper aromas.

2019 Vintage

The weather in early 2019 was dry and mild, with budbreak setting in at an early stage. May brought heavy rains and cooler temperatures that slowed the vines and brought growth.

Flowering progressed quickly in June, followed by a summer with hot temperatures and extremely dry periods.

Autumn weather was cooler than usual and we harvested the grapes in mid-September. The grapes showed only moderate sugar levels. 2019 has the potential of becoming one of the most elegant and subtle vintages seen in years. The wine has given rise to profound aromas especially minerality and apricot.
